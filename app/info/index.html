Lewis Nyman is a designer and a front-end developer.

Lewis has been working with the web for six years.

He graduated from Oxford Brookes university in 2010 with a first class honours degree in Law and Multimedia Computing

He lives in Zürich.

He can often be found writing, speaking, cycling, sprinting, thinking, and drinking.
